📖 Project Introduction & Usage Guide (Click to expand)

🎯 What is this tool?

This analyzer helps you understand the real-time performance characteristics of Azure Text-to-Speech (TTS) synthesis by measuring chunk delivery timing and sizes. It's essential for optimizing audio streaming applications that require low latency.

📊 Key Metrics Analyzed

  • First Chunk Latency: Time until the first audio chunk arrives (critical for perceived responsiveness)
  • Chunk Sizes: Distribution of audio chunk sizes throughout synthesis
  • Chunk Timing: When each chunk starts and completes receiving
  • Inter-Chunk Delays: Gaps between chunk deliveries (helps identify jitter)

💡 Tips for Best Results

  • Test with multiple sentences to get representative statistics
  • Try different output formats to compare latency characteristics
  • Use the history feature to compare different configurations
  • Export data for deeper analysis in Excel or other tools
  • Check inter-chunk delays to identify network jitter issues
